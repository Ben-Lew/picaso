picaso enables the: 1) computation of exoplanet and brown dwarf spectroscopy in transmission, emission or reflected light, 2) 1D climate modeling of brown dwarfs and exoplanets, 3) fitting spectroscopic data to models.

PICASO¶




Major Features¶

  • Reflected Light Spectroscopy of planets

  • Thermal Emission Spectroscopy of planets and brown dwarfs

  • Transit Spectroscopy of planets

  • Phase Curves of planets

  • 1D Climate modeling of planets and brown dwarfs

  • Fitting models to data
